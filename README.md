# Underweight-Overweight-cause-prediction
At yippee noodles factory, the final product is a noodle cake, the data contains the weights of the cakes, and other parameters on which the weight of the cake depends
The analysis analyses various factor with the standard deviation method and predicts the core reason of the underweight or overweight rejection of the final product ie. noodle cake
Underweight and overweight cause prediction of a final product in a noodles production line
